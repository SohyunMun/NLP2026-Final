Two loves I have, of comfort and despair,
That like two spirits do suggest me still.
My better angel is a man right fair,
Who looks up from heaven on you in glory;
He chides those whom he saw before him:
So far your shame does
not imprison his ghostly breath.
Your mother's love bears witness to this very thing
And her trust she has never so deep! whereupon most
